• Use to drive the ship
  • Press « P » to pause the game and « M » to mute the game.
  • Press « Space Key » to hit the enemy
  • The initial value of fuel is 100, which decreases by 1 every second. When the fuel value is 0, the game ends.
  • Touch the fallen bottle of fuel to add 15 points. Maximum amount of fuel is 100 points.
  • Hit an enemy ship to increase by 5 points. Planets must be hit twice, 10 points are added.
  • For hitting an associate, 10 points are reduced.
  • The game allows negative scores.
